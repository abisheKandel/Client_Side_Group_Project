Why feedback matters

Information about long waits, confusing booking steps, or missing features can help improve tools like this and reduce pressure on NHS services.

In a full system, feedback could be shared (anonymously) with NHS teams to support real service improvements.

Other ways to get help

  • For urgent medical advice: contact NHS 111.
  • For emergencies: call 999 immediately.
  • For non-urgent issues: contact your GP practice directly.
