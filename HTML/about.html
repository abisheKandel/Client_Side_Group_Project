About this prototype

This website is a student-built prototype that explores how digital tools could help people find and book NHS appointments with shorter waiting times while avoiding unsafe use of emergency services.

The problem

Patients often face long waiting times and are unsure which NHS service to use for their symptoms. This can lead to:

  • Missed or delayed care for people who need it sooner.
  • Unnecessary pressure on A&E and urgent services.
  • Frustration and confusion when booking appointments.

The aim

The aim of this prototype is to simulate a simple decision-support tool that:

  • Helps users check for obvious emergency warning signs.
  • Lists nearby NHS services and orders them by estimated waiting time.
  • Lets users book and manage basic appointment information in one place.

How it works

  • Assessment: a short questionnaire to highlight possible emergencies and direct users towards 999 or NHS 111 when needed.
  • Services: a local list of NHS providers with distance and simple estimated wait times, filtered by radius and sorted by shortest wait.
  • Appointments: a personal list where users can store, edit, and delete appointments linked to a chosen service.
  • Contact: a feedback form to collect ideas and issues related to waiting times or the booking journey.

Technical overview

  • Frontend built with HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Bootstrap is used for layout, responsiveness, and basic styling.
  • jQuery is used for simple DOM interactions and event handling.
  • Browser localStorage is used to simulate saving appointments, chosen services, assessment results, and feedback on the userâ€™s device.

Limitations and future improvements

This is not an official NHS service and has several important limitations:

  • It does not connect to real NHS systems or live waiting time data.
  • It does not replace professional triage or clinical judgement.
  • All information is stored only in the browser and is not secure enough for real patient records.

Future versions could:

  • Integrate with real NHS APIs and data feeds for accurate waiting time predictions.
  • Use stronger authentication and secure storage for patient information.
  • Test new ways of routing appointments to reduce waits and improve fairness across different patient groups.
